About us

OmegaTheme founded in 2010 in Hanoi, Vietnam, with close friends in university. After graduated few years we found our mission and chances to make something new and valued with web development. We started making themes, modules, plugins for open-source systems like Joomla, Wordpress, Magento, and Prestashop. In 2015 we released our first Shopify apps on the AppStore, and until now it's about over 35 apps with an average score of 4.7 from thousands of reviews and satisfied merchants.

Since 2017 we're only focused on making Shopify Apps. Our mission is to provide apps for help operation eCommerce shop easy for merchants, they are helping for increasing sales, running ads, building trust, or supporting end customers. With various major of work.

Our system architect always base on the high-end service provider on the world, 99.9999% uptime warrantied, fast, and reliable.
